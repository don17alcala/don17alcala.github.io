How is my semester going?

This semester has been going surprisingly well so far. I have been trying to keep my head above water, which is not always easy. I was initially nervous about taking on too much, starting a new job, moving, and starting my semester all within the same month. As with most things in life stressing out about the situation rarely helps. What I did was take it one situation at a time so that issues did not get compounded. It’s amazing how fast time is going and right now that’s not a bad thing. I will be happy to with school for a while. It has been an amazing journey, but I think I have had enough school for a while. What I did last weekend!

After the hustle and bustle of the work week I try to take a day to do something I enjoy. After sleeping in a bit Saturday morning, I met up with a friend of mine and went to the movies where we saw Venom. I was a little up in the air about the film, but it turned out to be well done and I would recommend it for a midafternoon movie, not really work $18.
